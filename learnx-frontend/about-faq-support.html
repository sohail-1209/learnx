About LearnX

Our Mission

LearnX is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform dedicated to connecting individuals who want to learn new skills with those who are passionate about teaching them. Our mission is to foster a collaborative and accessible learning environment where knowledge is shared freely or through fair exchange.

We believe that everyone has valuable skills to share, and everyone has the potential to learn. By removing traditional barriers and focusing on direct interaction, LearnX empowers individuals to expand their horizons and build a supportive learning community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does LearnX work?

LearnX connects learners with mentors for one-on-one or group sessions. You can search for skills you want to learn or list skills you can teach. Sessions can be paid or based on skill bartering.

What kind of skills can I learn or teach?

You can learn or teach a wide range of skills, including coding, design, music, languages, fitness, business, and more. If you have a skill you want to share, you can create a listing.

How do I become a mentor?

To become a mentor, simply sign up for an account and select the "Teacher" role. You can then create listings for the skills you want to teach and set your availability and session details.

How do I book a session with a mentor?

Browse the skill marketplace or use the search bar to find mentors in the skill you're interested in. On the mentor's profile or session listing, you'll find an option to book a session. You can choose the session type (paid or barter) and preferred time.

How do payments or bartering work?

Mentors can set a price for their sessions, or offer sessions for bartering (exchanging skills). Our platform facilitates secure payments for paid sessions. Bartering agreements are handled between the users.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, LearnX is a web-based platform. We are exploring options for a mobile application in the future.
